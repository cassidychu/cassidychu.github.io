The Software Developer and Designer

Hi! My name is Cassidy Chu! I am a student at San Jose State University studying Software Engineering and will be graduating in Fall 2024. My academic and current internship experiences have prepared me in data structures and algorithms and understanding databases. I have concentrated on programming websites and developing test cases. I have worked in various groups for the past 5 years developing websites and more recently mobile applications using JavaScript/Python/PHP and implementing tools/frameworks such as Figma, Firebase, React, Expo, and Swift.


Through these projects, I was able to utilize what I have learned from the Google UX Professional Design course through Coursera. Obtaining the certification allowed me to understand user pain points, conduct usability studies, research competitive audits, and design low and high fidelity wireframes providing a better user experience.


A little about me outside of my academic careers is I enjoy the outdoors. I recently got to go backpacking for the first time in 2023 at Big Sur and enjoy camping. My latest camping trip was at Yosemite, but a goal of mine is to one day visit all of the National Parks in America. During the winter, I spend my time snowboarding.


On my free time, I like to paint, bake, play video games, go fishing, or walk around my neighborhood with my trash picker.


I am open to exploring opportunities in the Software Engineering and UI/UX field.
